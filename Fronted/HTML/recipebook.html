1. Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, add it to the pan with pancetta, and toss well.
  5. Remove from heat, add the egg mixture, and toss quickly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.

2. Chocolate Chip Cookies

Deliciously soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour and baking soda, then gradually add to the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden.

3. Chicken Curry

A flavorful and spicy chicken curry that's easy to make and perfect with rice or naan.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  4. Add the curry powder and tomato puree, and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in coconut milk, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
